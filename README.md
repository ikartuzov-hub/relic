## The problem
A good find loses its value when it's buried in a generic, one-language listing. Discoveries deserve a presentation that's curated and native to the reader — not another flat catalog.

## The build
Relic is another product on the same SeedWave engine, pointed at curated finds. It carries the standard surface — multilingual UI, two themes, and the SeedWave funnel — which makes it one more piece of evidence that the engine travels across domains.

## Stack
`SeedWave engine` · `multilingual (RU/EN/PT/ES/DE)` · `two-theme UI (day/night)` · `GitHub Pages`
